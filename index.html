MORE ABOUT US

We help you transition seamlessly

AbiBio-degradable is a brand that was established in 2025. We are pioneer in biodegradable polymer compound products which are derived from cassava, corn, and other industrial natural starches. Starting with commitments to make a positive impact on the environment, our company has grown into a leading player in the field of biopolymer materials in Nepal. We are committed to fostering sustainability through our innovation of biodegradable polymer to combat plastic pollution. We successfully fulfill local demand and ultimately achieve success in exporting to various countries in Asia, Middle East, America, Europe, Africa, and Australia.
